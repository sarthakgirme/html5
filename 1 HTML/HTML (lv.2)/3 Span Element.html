span Element

span is a Generic Conainer used to hold other HTML Elements or Group of Elements together.

span is a Inline Element.
It doesn't Occupy the whole Line.

Inside div tag :

Apple Orange
Pineapple

Apple and orange is inside the div tag. and pineapple is Outside the div tag.

Inside span tag :

Apple Orange Pineapple

Apple and orange is inside the span tag. and pineapple is Outside the span tag.
