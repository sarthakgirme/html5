Semantic & Non-Semantic Markups

All the Tags are usually Categorised into two Marksups i.e., Semantic and Non-Semantic Markups.

Semantic Markup

In this Markup, the Tags are Easily Understandable just by looking at them or reading.

Example : b Tag, img Tag, p Tag, etc.

Non-Semantic Markup

In this Markup, the Tags are Complex to Understand just by looking at them or reading.

Example : div Tag, span Tag.

  • Meaningful | Layout becomes more Structured.
  • SEO Friendly (Search Engine Optimization). | Our website will Improve Ranking wise.
  • Readable. | Screen Readable.

Semantic Tags

  • Header (will give clear insight about my web-page)
  • Main (for main contain of my website)
  • Footer
  • Nav (navigation tags)
  • Article
  • Section (to group together related containt | like Dev Tag)
  • Aside (link of an another movie of an actor on page which is not directly related to our web-page but indirectly.)
